Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

This Refund & Cancellation Policy (the "Policy") describes the terms under which a confirmed booking made through the Book My Dreamland mobile application (the "App") may be cancelled, modified, or refunded. By completing a booking through the App you agree to this Policy in addition to our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. The App is operated by Humble Solutions ("we", "us", or "our").

1. Cancellation Window — Per Room

Each room listed in the App carries its own cancellation terms set by the property. You can review these terms on the room detail screen before booking, and again on the checkout screen before payment. The terms always specify:

  • Free Cancellation Window — the number of hours before check-in within which you may cancel the booking and receive a full refund of the room charge.
  • Partial Refund Percentage — the share of the room charge refunded when a cancellation is made outside the free cancellation window but before the check-in time.
  • Policy Note — any additional terms specific to the room or property (e.g. peak-season exceptions).

The terms shown on the checkout screen at the moment of payment are the terms that apply to your booking.

2. How to Cancel

  • Open the App and go to Bookings.
  • Select the booking you wish to cancel.
  • Tap Cancel Booking and confirm.

Cancellations made through the App are processed immediately. The applicable refund is calculated based on the room's cancellation policy and the time remaining until your scheduled check-in.

3. No-Show

If you do not arrive at the property on or before your scheduled check-in date and have not cancelled the booking in advance through the App, the booking will be marked as a no-show. No-show bookings are not eligible for any refund, regardless of the room's cancellation terms.

4. Early Check-Out

Refunds for early check-out from a confirmed and active stay are at the sole discretion of the property and are not guaranteed. Any approved partial refund for unused nights will be coordinated directly between you and the property; the App is not party to such adjustments unless an explicit early-checkout fee is configured on the room.

5. Refund Processing Time

When a refund is due to you under this Policy:

  • We initiate the refund within 2 business days of receiving your cancellation.
  • The refund is returned to the same payment method you used to book.
  • Depending on your bank or card issuer, the credit may take an additional 5 to 10 business days to appear on your statement.

If you have not received the refund after 10 business days from the date of cancellation, please contact us at the email address below with your booking reference.

6. Tax, Fees, and Surcharges

Refunds are calculated against the room charge as displayed at booking. Statutory taxes (such as GST) collected on the refunded amount are refunded proportionally. Convenience fees, payment-gateway charges, and any non-refundable surcharges explicitly disclosed before payment are not refunded.

7. Modifications to a Booking

Date changes, guest-count changes, and other modifications to a confirmed booking are subject to the same cancellation terms as the original booking. If a modification cannot be accommodated, you may cancel the booking under this Policy and rebook for the new dates.

8. Force Majeure

If you are unable to honour a confirmed booking due to a verifiable event beyond your reasonable control (natural disaster, government travel restriction, medical emergency with supporting documentation), we will work with the property in good faith to issue a full or partial refund or to reschedule the stay. Such accommodations are not guaranteed and are evaluated case-by-case.

9. Disputes and Grievances

If you believe a cancellation or refund has not been processed correctly under this Policy, please contact us at the address below within 30 days of the cancellation date. We will respond within 7 business days.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Changes apply only to bookings made on or after the revision date; existing bookings remain governed by the Policy in effect at the time of payment.
